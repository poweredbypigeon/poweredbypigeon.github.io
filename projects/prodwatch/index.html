ProdWatch

CV-powered system tracks if you're focused or not.

PROJECT DETAILS

UPDATE HISTORY

FEATURES


ProdWatch is an entry me and my team submitted to the NewHacks hackathon. It uses computer vision techniques and a convolutional neural network (CNN) to detect if you appear tired or not being focused, and then prompts you with a message to either look at the screen (if you’re not focused) or take a break (if you’re tired).
