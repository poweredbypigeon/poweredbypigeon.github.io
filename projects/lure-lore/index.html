INTRO

LureLore is an app created for fishing enthusiasts who want to know more about fishing or keep a collection of fish they caught.


Technologies used; MERN stack, Gemini API (AI backend), Leaflet API (mapping), three.js (aquarium)


We were chosen as one of 12 finalists for Hack the North, and gave a presentation to everyone who attended (>1000 participants).
